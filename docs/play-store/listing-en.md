## Full description (4000 chars max)

```
⚙️ Calibration Calculator — built for instrument & control engineers in the field.

Two purpose-built tools in one focused, offline app:

▌LOOP CALCULATOR
Map any process variable to a 4-20mA (or any other) output signal.
• Configurable input / output ranges
• Quick 0% · 25% · 50% · 75% · 100% checkpoint buttons
• Live bar-graph with real-time percentage readout
• Decimal-precision input with a fast, glove-friendly numeric keypad
• Perfect for verifying temperature, pressure, flow and level transmitters

▌POSITIONER CALCULATOR
Calculate exact DCS settings for valve positioner calibration.
• Enter physical zero / span mA readings from the field
• Set the desired DCS percentage range (e.g. -0.5% to 99.5%)
• Two-zone visualization for the Zero Zone (-5% to 5%) and Span Zone (95% to 105%)
• Instant 0% / 100% DCS mA results — no spreadsheet, no guesswork

▌WHY ENGINEERS CHOOSE IT
• 100% offline — works in the plant, in the substation, on the rig
• Dark, high-contrast UI tuned for outdoor and indoor lighting
• Compact single-screen layout optimized for modern Android phones (Galaxy S24/S25)
• Haptic feedback on every keypress so you feel each entry with gloves on
• Portrait-only — no accidental landscape flips while holding a multimeter
• Free, with a single non-intrusive banner ad to keep the app maintained

▌DESIGNED FOR
Process control engineers · Instrumentation technicians · DCS commissioning teams
Plant operators · MEP engineers · Industrial automation specialists
Field service engineers working with Yokogawa, Siemens, Honeywell, ABB, Emerson DCS systems

▌WHAT'S INCLUDED
• Fully offline calculation engine
• Persistent settings (last range and value are remembered between sessions)
• No account, no signup, no cloud — your inputs never leave your device
• Banner advertisements via Google AdMob

▌PRIVACY
The app does not collect personally identifying information. Calculator state is
stored locally on your device only. Google AdMob may collect advertising
identifiers and approximate IP-based location to serve and measure ads — see the
full Privacy Policy in the About dialog or at the URL listed below.
```
